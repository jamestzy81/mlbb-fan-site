Heroes of MLBB

Each hero in MLBB has unique abilities and playstyles, making every battle exciting and dynamic.

Hero Classes:

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang (MLBB) features an extensive roster of heroes, each with unique abilities and roles that make gameplay diverse and exciting. Here’s an overview of the hero classes and some popular heroes within the game:

  1. Tank

    2. ● Role: Frontline protectors with high durability, shielding their teammates and initiating fights.

    ● Examples:

    ➜ Tigreal:A tank with excellent crowd control, capable of disrupting enemies and protecting allies.

    ➜ Grock:Known for his ability to block enemy movement with walls and high survivability.

  2. Fighter:

    3. ● Role: Versatile damage dealers and off-tanks, often excelling in melee combat.

    ● Examples:

    ➜ Chou:A fighter with high mobility and crowd control, great for picking off enemies.

    ➜ Freya:A melee fighter with devastating burst damage and sustain.

  3. Assassin:

    4. ● Role: Specializes in quick, high-damage bursts to eliminate squishy targets.

    ● Examples:

    ➜ Gusion:Known for his fast combo execution and magical burst damage.

    ➜ ling:A nimble assassin with unparalleled mobility, capable of traversing walls.

  4. Marksman:

    5. ● Role: Long-range damage dealers with a focus on sustained DPS, ideal for taking down objectives and enemies in late-game scenarios.

    ● Examples:

    ➜ Layla: A beginner-friendly marksman with long-range attacks and high damage potential.

    ➜ Kimmy:A hybrid marksman-mage with unique auto-aiming mechanics.

  5. Mage:

    6. ● Role: High burst magical damage dealers, often pivotal in team fights with crowd control and area-of-effect abilities.

    ● Examples:

    ➜ Eudora:Known for her high burst damage and effective crowd control.

    ➜ Esmeralda: A tanky mage who excels in absorbing shields and dealing consistent magic damage.

  6. Support::

    7. ● Role: Helps the team by healing, buffing allies, or disabling enemies.

    ● Examples:

    ➜ Angela:A support hero with healing abilities and a unique skill to attach to an ally.

    ➜ Rafaela:Heals and boosts allies' movement speed while dealing magic damage to enemies.

Popular Heroes in MLBB:

  1. Zilong (Fighter):
    A swift warrior with a spear, Zilong is known for his ability to dive into battles and execute enemies with high attack speed and mobility.
  2. Alucard (Fighter/Assassin):
    A hybrid fighter and assassin, Alucard excels in lifesteal and is perfect for players who enjoy dueling.
  3. Harith (Mage):
    A time-traveling mage with high mobility, Harith can dominate mid-game team fights with area-of-effect magic damage.
  4. Lesley (Marksman):
    A stealthy sniper who can deal critical hits from a distance, Lesley is perfect for picking off enemies safely.
  5. Franco (Tank):
    Famous for his "Iron Hook," Franco is a tank who can pull enemies out of position and initiate team fights effectively.

    6. Fun fact:

    MLBB regularly introduces new heroes, balancing the meta and keeping the game dynamic. Players can experiment with various heroes to find their preferred playstyle, making each match unique and engaging.
